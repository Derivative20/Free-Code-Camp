Golden Rice Project

The ground-breaking research that will save millions of children from blindness

Background

The Golden Rice Project was research conducted in late 2015 to help with the crisis occurring in Africa; Vitamin A deficiency. This initiative began the Rockefeller Foundation brought together multiple professors from around the Globe and allowed them to conduct research on bio-fortification. This allowed them to achieve their ultimate goal of genetically motifying rice to deliver Vitamin A to over 200 million people.
